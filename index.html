Participant Information and Consent

Thank you for taking part in this study.

In this task, you will be asked to browse a simulated online shopping website and answer a survey, imagining that you are considering purchasing a product. Please interact with the website as you normally would when shopping online. You will interact with the simulated website and be asked to buy products with different shipping options.

During the task, you will: 1. Browse the available products. 2. Select a product that you would consider purchasing. 3. Review the delivery options available at checkout. 3. Choose the delivery option you would prefer. 4. Complete a form with your preferences and answer questions about your experience.

You are invited to take part in a research study exploring how delivery options and website design influence online shopping decisions.

Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time before submitting your responses.

No personally identifiable information will be collected. Responses will be used solely for academic research purposes.

The study should take approximately 3–5 minutes to complete.

By clicking "I Agree and Start", you confirm that:

  • You are at least 18 years old.
  • You have read the information above.
  • You voluntarily agree to participate.
  • Your data will be stored for 10 years.
